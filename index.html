Imm Thai

Address:

2068 University Ave
Berkeley, CA 94704
Downtown Berkeley

Phone:

(510) 898-1123

Yelp:

https://www.yelp.com/biz/imm-thai-street-food-berkeley?osq=imm+thai

Review:

Relatively cheap considering it's location in downtown Berkeley. It gets pretty crowded at peak hours. Seats are pretty closed together so probably not convenient for big groups. Decent dishes with a wide variety. Recommend dishes: Pad See Eew (as always),chicken wings and mango sticky rice.

Thai Basil

Address:

2519 Durant Ave
Ste G
Berkeley, CA 94704
b/t Bowditch St & Telegraph Ave
Telegraph Ave

Phone:

(510) 548-6692

Yelp:

https://www.yelp.com/biz/thai-basil-cuisine-berkeley

Review:

Located in the "Asian Ghetto" in Southside of Berkeley Campus.It's probably the cheapest Thai Cuisine in the area. IMPORTANT: THEY ONLY TAKE CASH!. No tips required. Good place for students to go (as we are all poor). They have seats inside but very limited; hence, not a good place for groups. Recommend dishes: Pad See Eew, mango sticky rice, yellow curry

Berkeley Social Club

Address:

2050 University Ave
Berkeley, CA 94704
b/t Milvia St & Shattuck Ave
Downtown Berkeley

Phone:

(510) 900-5858

Yelp:

https://www.yelp.com/biz/berkeley-social-club-berkeley?osq=Berkeley+Social

Review:

Located in Downtown Berkeley (close to the Northside). Price range is a bit high for students. Specialty in Korean-American fusion food. Interior is nice and as plenty of spaces- good for big groups and family dining. Dishes are pretty nice and large in portion. Recommend dishes: Fried Chicken w Waffles, Beef Soup with glass noodle.
